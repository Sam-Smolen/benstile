About me

My name is Ben. I have been involved in the construction industry for 7 years now. I started as an apprentice union carpenter. Spending years learning the art of concrete. I worked on the light rail project, the new good addition in Kirkland and multiple high rises. Concrete is a favorite of mine. After that I went residential, learning skills such as tile and other remodeling specialties and I quickly fell in love. After a few years I needed the next chapter. Something more fulfilling. So I started this company, Ben's Tile and More looking to start my journey to create beautiful works of art in peoples houses. Tile is a personal favorite becuase of the art it brings to a room.

Our Services

We specialize in bathroom, kitchen and floor tile installation and remodels of all sizes. Whether you are looking for a unique look in your kitchen with a new back splash or a new tile shower, we cover it all. At Ben's tile and More color and style is something we take seriously. Our top priority is that our tile always adds life to any space. We offer style advice if needed!
